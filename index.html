A little about RTS .

The original mission was made by KaRRiLLioN in 2002-2003 in ofp , he remade the mission for arma1. But desided to move on to different things, So for some time I've been trying to bring RTS back to the arma world. I failed in arma2, but in Arma3 I found a BIS forum member Zenophon who was and still is developing his own framework and was interested in all aspects of mission making and coding. (and here we are)
